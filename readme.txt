Help stop Joseph Kony by putting this ribbon on your website.

== Description ==

Joseph Kony is one of the world's worst war criminals. Show your support to the intentional effort to arrest him, disarm the LRA an bring the child soldiers home.

A black ribbon with the words "Kony 2012" will be placed near the top right corner of your website. The ribbon links to the Invisible Children | Kony 2012 (http://www.kony2012.com) website.

Look at the screenshot below to see how the ribbon looks.

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin zip archive.
2. Extract it in your `wp-content/plugins` folder or upload via admin panel.
3. Browse to your plugins section and active the plugin.
